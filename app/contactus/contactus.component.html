What is the product derived from?

This is the first question to ask yourself. In other words, what’s in it, and where does it come from? Is it a natural source, like plants, animals, minerals, and marine sources? Or is it derived from petrochemicals?

How is it processed?

Just because ingredients are derived naturally doesn’t mean they’re processed naturally. Ingredients can be processed physically or chemically.
